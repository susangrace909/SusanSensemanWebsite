Operations Tracker (Live)

A Power BI Project analyzing workforce performance, efficiency, and utilization across departments.

  • Role: Data Analyst
  • Tools: Power BI, Excel
  • Date: 2025

Watch the Dashboard Demo

This dashboard was designed to help operations managers monitor team performance metrics such as efficiency, downtime, and goal achievement. By integrating KPI cards, trend visuals, and department comparisons, it provides a centralized view of productivity and operational health.

Problem

Leaders lacked visibility into workforce efficiency and needed a cohesive way to assess department performance, identify downtime causes, and track goal attainment in real time.

Approach

Using a curated dataset of operational metrics, I cleaned and modeled the data in Power BI — creating DAX measures for efficiency, utilization, and performance variance. I then designed a unified dashboard with custom color themes, interactive slicers, and visual hierarchy for a clear, brand-aligned presentation.

Outcome

The finished dashboard delivers actionable insights into efficiency trends and operational bottlenecks. Users can filter by department, shift, or performance rating to analyze results from multiple perspectives. The design emphasizes clarity and usability — mirroring my personal branding style and aligning with the clean, eucalyptus-and-charcoal palette featured across my portfolio.

Highlights

  • Built custom DAX measures to calculate efficiency gaps, goal achievement %, and utilization.
  • Designed KPI cards and comparative visuals with a consistent aesthetic.
  • Created a dynamic overview that consolidates multiple analysis pages.
  • Applied accessibility-focused color design and layout balance.
